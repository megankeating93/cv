Villa Maria Academy High School

Villa Maria Academy, a Catholic college-preparatory school for girls, rooted in the charism of the Sisters, Servants of the Immaculate Heart of Mary, empowers young women to lead lives of spiritual growth, intellectual inquiry and Christian service. Villa Maria Academy High School is an independent private school located in Malvern, Pennsylvania.
